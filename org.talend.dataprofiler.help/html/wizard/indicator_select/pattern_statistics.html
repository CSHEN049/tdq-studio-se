Pattern frequency statistics:

  Offers indicators to analyze the structure of your data. It replaces all lowercase alphabetic characters by "a", all uppercase alphabetic characters by "A" and all numeric characters by "9".

  For example, a UK postal code such as "SL6 1NJ" would give this pattern: "AA9 9AA".

  The pattern frequency table shows the most frequent patterns in your data and how many times they appear.

  The pattern low frequency table shows the less frequent patterns and how many times they appear.
