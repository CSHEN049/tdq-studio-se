Soundex frequency statistics:
-------------------------------------
  Offers indicators to analyze the phonetic frequency of your data. It is useful to identify spelling errors.
  For example, "abod" and "adobe" will produce the same sound although they are different. These indicators will show that "adobe" has two distinct spellings.
  The soundex frequency table shows the most frequent sounds in your data and how many times they appear.
  The soundex low frequency table shows the less frequent sounds and how many times they appear.
