Advanced statistics:
-------------------------------------
  Offers other indicators for a more detailed analysis of your data.
  The frequency table shows the most frequent values and how many times they appear.
  The low frequency table shows the less frequent values and how many times they appear. It is useful to detect data quality issues.
  The mode is the most probable value, the value that appear most often in your data.
